Overview

For All Mankind is a TV series created by Apple and Sony that has won a large number of awards throughout its 4 seasons. However, the distribution of the series and its popularity among average users does not do justice to the distinctions it has.

Goal

Design a website that generates good user feedback and achieves a good conversion rate of Apple Tv+ subscriptions.

Main challenge

How might we make users find the series interesting when they are exploring the website?

Outcomes

A usability test was done followed by a survey with 5 users to see their behavior and opinion of the product.
