Overview

Santiago Gimenez Fan is a website that offers information about the career of football player Santiago Gimenez, his stats, matches and news. The project is just starting and seeks to build trust in users and grow its audience on different platforms.

Goal

Create a site that informs users about current events in Santiago. Generate confidence in the users that visit the site and get them to watch the videos, statistics and news.

Problems

News comes from different media and many times it can be false.
There is no clear and simple way to know Santiago 's statistics and current events in depth.
The variety of sites offering news can be overwhelming for users.

Tools

Customer avatars were created from secondary research, user journeys, competitive analysis, problem statement and solutions were devised with HMWs. (Not all methods used are shown, please contact me for that)

Main challenges

How might we Inform about the results that Santiago has in its games in a simple and easy to access way?
How might we present statistics in a way that is easy for the user to understand?

Solution

Task Flows of the main tasks and a sitemap were created to have a clear and simple organization.
For the visual design I focused on something accessible and that showed personality.

Organization and structure

Create a site that is easy to navigate thanks to an intuitive content organization.

Trust

Generate confidence to users who visit the site, either when they read an article or when they are exploring a screen.

In-depth statistics

Provide users with complete and in-depth Santiago statistics that are easy to understand and navigate.

Create an ecosystem

The site will serve as an entry and distribution point for other social media.
