Overview

With the release of Apple Vision Pro, the opportunity to create new interactions for users led me to design an experience focused on Tennis, bringing the Set Winner app to the AR/VR world.

Goal

The goal was simple: adapt the Tennis app I had already made to the Apple Vision Pro ecosystem, ensuring that the experience is intuitive and accessible to users, without causing fatigue and generating satisfaction in them.

Main challenge

How might we create an immersive Tennis viewing experience without causing user fatigue?
